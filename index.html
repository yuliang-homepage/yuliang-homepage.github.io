Yu liang 梁 堉

Yu Liang (pronounced as yoo-lyahng) is currently an Assistant Professor of the College of Computer Science at Beijing University of Technology, China. He received a Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the School of Computer Science and Engineering and the Shen Yuan Honors College (an elite program), Beihang University, China, supervised by Prof. Wenjun Wu. He also received a B.Sc. degree in 2017 from Beihang University.
