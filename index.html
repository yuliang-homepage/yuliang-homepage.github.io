Research

I am interested in machine learning for education.

Now my research mainly includes:
  • Educational Data Mining(EDM)
  • Student Models for cognitive explanation(e.g. knowledge tracing)
  • Automated Program Repair(APR) for student code
  • Intelligent Tutoring System(ITS)
  • Artificial Intelligence of Things(AIoT)
