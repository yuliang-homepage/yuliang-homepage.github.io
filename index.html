Yu liang 梁 堉

Yu Liang, pronounced as "yoo (falling tone)- lyahng (rising tone)", is currently an Assistant Professor of the College of Computer Science, Beijing University of Technology, China. He received a Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the School of Computer Science and Engineering and the Shen Yuan Honors College (an elite program), Beihang University, China, supervised by Prof. Wenjun Wu. He also received a B.Sc. degree in 2017 from Beihang University.

Email: yuliang@bjut.edu.cn | yliang@buaa.edu.cn

Research

My research interests include artificial intelligence, deep learning, human–computer interaction, multimodal learning, and educational technologies, with applications in intelligent perception, cognitive modeling, and adaptive tutoring systems.
