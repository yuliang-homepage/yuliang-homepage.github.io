Yu Liang 梁 堉

I am currently an Assistant Professor of the College of Computer Science, Beijing University of Technology, China. My research interests lie in multimodal artificial intelligence, with a focus on intelligent perception, human–computer interaction, cognitive modeling, and educational technologies.

Prior to this, I received my Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the School of Computer Science and Engineering and the Shen Yuan Honors College (an elite program), Beihang University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Wenjun Wu. I also obtained my B.Sc. degree from Beihang University in 2017.
