Research

My research interest is Artificial Intelligence in Education. My research is focused on using AI, machine learning, and related technologies to understand student learning and help them learn better and faster.

Now my research mainly includes:
  • Educational Data Mining (EDM)
  • Student Models for cognitive explanation (e.g. knowledge tracing)
  • Reinforcement Learning (RL) for Learning Path Recommendation
  • Automated Program Repair (APR) for student code
  • Intelligent Tutoring System (ITS)
  • Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT) in Education
